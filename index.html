God has written eternity in our hearts. Eternity is the true substance, this temporal life is but a shadow. God inhabits eternity and He has extended eternal life through Jesus Christ our Lord. Our finite minds tend to dwell on the day to day because it is easier to see and focus on, plus we have daily needs, but the Word of God promises to meet those needs, when we place Him first place. Salvation through Jesus Christ is a win win situation, our needs for beyond our last day and our needs for today are met in Him.
